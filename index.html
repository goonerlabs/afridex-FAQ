Guide to account functions

registration

Open the AFRIDEX exchange website in your favorite web browser (afridex.io)
1. Click on the register button on the top right side of the landing page.

2. Alternatively, you can click on the Get started button.

3. Fill in all required fields, including the fields for the email and password, and verify the password.

4. complete the captcha, view and agree to our terms of use, and click on the register button. a link would be sent to your registered email address, please click on this link to verify your email address.

a. If you do not find this link in your inbox, please check your promotions or spam folder, after which you should whitelist AFRIDEX official email address by moving the message from your spam folder to your inbox by right-clicking on the message, hovering with your mouse over to the move to link and by clicking on the inbox option.

b. if the message is in your promotions folder, right-click on the message, hover over the label link, unselect the promotions option and then click on apply. the message would then be moved to your inbox and subsequent emails from the AFRIDEX exchange would be sent to your inbox.

KYC verification

1. login to the AFRIDEX exchange by clicking on the login button on the top right-hand side of the landing page.

2. a one-time password (OTP) would be sent to your registered email address, please copy and paste it in the input field provided to add OTP.

3. Click on profile and then the account settings.

4. you would be redirected to a settings page with each step/tier of the verification procedure listed.

5. complete each step of the verification process by filling out each of the required forms.

KYC verification

To reset your password;
1. If you aren't logged in yet, Navigate to the login page click on the forgot password link.

2. if you are already logged in, click on profile and then account settings to navigate to the settings page. there, you would find the option to change your password.

3. Enter your registered email address and a link to reset your password would be sent to your email address.

Deposits and Withdrawals

deposits

1. After logging in to the AFRIDEX exchange, click on buy crypto at the top left-hand side of the landing page and you would be redirected to the buy crypto page.

2. you can then deposit assets on the AFRIDEX exchange you can either use the P2P feature (fiat). (which would be described in another section dedicated to P2P) or can deposit from any wallet where your assets are stored (crypto). The procedure for the latter would be described in this section.

3. click on the funds dropdown menu on the top right-hand side of the page and then the deposit/withdraw link you would then be redirected to the Deposits/withdrawals page.

4. click on deposit

5. click on the dropdown menu to select the token/ coin you want to deposit.

6. You would have the option of either copying your AFRIDEX deposit address and pasting it in the wallet you are making a deposit from e.g binance, metamask, or trustwallet or scanning the QR code to grab the address from the said wallet to make your deposit.

7. please always ensure that the correct/applicable blockchain/network is used when making deposits and withdrawals.

withdrawals

1. After logging in to the AFRIDEX exchange click on the buy crypto on the top left-hand side of the landing page and you would be redirected to the buy crypto page.

2. to withdraw assets from the AFRIDEX exchange you can either use the P2P feature. (which would be described in another section dedicated to P2P) or can withdraw to any other wallet of your choosing e.g binace, metamask, or trustwallet. The procedure for the latter would be described in this section.

3. click on the funds dropdown menu and then the deposit/withdraw on the link you would then be redirected to the Deposits/withdrawals page.

4. click on withdraw.

5. click on the dropdown menu to select the token/coin you want to withdraw.

6. you would have to add the withdrawal address. to do this, click on manage addresses, paste the address of the wallet you are making a withdrawal to e.g binance, metamask, or trustwallet in the input field, and fill other input fields, save it and then select the address

7. put the specific amount of the asset you want to withdraw in the input field.

8. an OTP would be sent to your registered email address, copy and paste the OTP and click on the submit button. if you don't find the OTP in your inbox, please check your spam or promotions folder.

9. please always ensure that the correct/applicable blockchain/network is used when making deposits and withdrawals.

P2P

The peer to peer trading feature can be used to convert fiat to crypto and vice versa

posting a trade

1. login to the afridex.io.

2. click on the assets button and then the P2P link on the top right of the landing page. Alternatively, you can click on the buy crypto button on the top left side of the landing page.

3. click on post a trade, choose either to buy online or sell online from the options made available and then enter your preferred payment method alongside other required information such as your bank details and other required fields

4. step3 above has been previously completed, you can go straight ahead and click the quick buy/ quick sell button depending on what you intend to do.

5. To cancel an order, click on the profile icon and then my offers link. you would be redirected to a page whereby you can view your offers and cancel buy or sell orders.
you can also change your payment method by clicking on the profile icon and the payment methods link.

Buying/ Selling crypto

1. after navigating to the p2p page, click on the quick buy or quick sell link depending on whichever applies to you. fill the required fields including your preferred payment method, your local fiat currency, and your location.
then click on the search button, a list of sellers/ buyers in your location would be made available alongside all necessary information about the buyer/seller such as information about the number of trades they have completed, ratings, amongst others.

2. when you have decided on a buyer/seller click on the buy/sell button on the far right of your screen attached to the particular buyer/seller. After which you may complete the transaction.

3. Sellers must always provide the buyer with ample time to make payment and buyers must always provide sellers with ample time to confirm the transaction. you can also contact the counterparty by clicking on the message icon on the top right section of the screen to use our in-app chat feature or by sending an SMS or making a phone call using the number provided to hasten the process. sellers must always ensure that the buyer has made payment and confirm the bank transaction before releasing the tokens.
Should any dispute ever occur, please contact AFRIDEX exchange via the live chat feature or send an email to support@afridex.io.

spot Trading

spot trading (Desktop)

1. login to the AFRIDEX exchange, click exchange on the top left side of the landing page.

2. Choose whichever base currency that you want to use to trade on the right of your screen and then either click on a trading pair or search for the currency that you want to buy. For example, if you want to exchange USDT for BTC, your base currency is USDT so you click on USDT and then search for the BTC/USDT pair.

3. After selecting a currency pair, you can then place a market order (at current market price; the trade would be executed instantly) or limit order (at any price you chose; the trade would be executed immediately your limit order price is triggered).

spot trading (Mobile)

1. If you are using the mobile web application, click on the menu button on the topmost right section of the landing page and then the exchange link.
2. to chose a base currency or change the currency pair, click on the market tab. To execute a trade, click on the trade tab.

3. After selecting a currency pair, you can then place a market order (at current market price; the trade would be executed instantly) or limit order (at any price you chose; the trade would be executed immediately your limit order price is triggered).

Security

At a minimum, the AFRIDEX exchange requires two-factor authentication (2FA) via email to log in or to withdraw funds.

Aditional Two Factor Authentication

An additional layer of security may also be added via SMS and google authenticator. To enable this feature, click on the profile and then account settings. you would be redirected to the settings page. here you can enable google authenticator by clicking on the google authenticator card and then you have the option to either scan the QR code with your google authenticator APP or by copying the authenticator key and pasting it in your google authenticator APP

Next of kin

The AFRIDEX exchange provides a next of kin feature, where users can add their next of kin's information. The user would be contacted if their account has been inactive for 18 months and if the user fails to respond, their next of kin would be contacted. To enable this feature, click on profile and then account settings to be redirected to the settings page and then click on the next of kin card. all you have to do is simply fill the required fields to enable this feature.
